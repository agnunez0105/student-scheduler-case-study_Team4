Project Overview

Booked & Busy is a UX group project focused on helping college students manage their time between classes, study sessions, extracurriculars, and social life.

This project was completed by Arianna Nunez, Cecilia Bilski, and Alexander Clarke. It was inspired by real student struggles with burnout, time conflicts, and decision-making under pressure.

Problem

Students often struggle to balance academic responsibilities with extracurricular involvement. This leads to stress, burnout, and difficulty managing their time effectively.

  • Conflicting schedules between classes, study time, and clubs
  • Lack of structured planning tools
  • Overwhelming workload during busy weeks
  • Difficulty making decisions under pressure
Design Goal: Help students manage their time while supporting both productivity and campus involvement.

Role

I contributed to research, persona development, and design planning for this project.

  • Participated in research and interviews
  • Helped organize affinity diagrams
  • Contributed to persona development
  • Worked on feature planning
  • Collaborated on prototype direction

Process

Research & Affinity Mapping

We analyzed student experiences and used affinity mapping to identify patterns related to stress, workload, and scheduling challenges.

Persona Development

We created a persona named Ella, representing students trying to balance academics and social involvement.

Design Ideas

  • Calendar integration
  • Daily schedule view
  • Automatic study-time insertion
  • Club selection and confirmation
  • Social schedule sharing

Prototype

The prototype demonstrates onboarding, schedule creation, and automated study planning.

Outcome

The final concept is a scheduling tool that helps students plan their time more effectively and reduce stress.

  • Combines academic and social scheduling
  • Automatically suggests study time
  • Improves time visibility
  • Supports better balance
